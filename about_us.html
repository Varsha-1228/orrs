At ORRS (Online Railway Reservation System), we aim to revolutionize the way people plan and book their railway journeys. Our platform provides a secure, efficient, and user-friendly interface for passengers to search trains, check availability, and reserve tickets online. Developed using PHP and MySQL, ORRS integrates a robust backend with a clean frontend to deliver a seamless reservation experience. The system is designed to minimize booking errors, streamline ticket management, and reduce the hassle of long queues at railway stations. Whether you're a daily commuter or planning a long journey, ORRS is built to support your travel needs with: Real-time train schedules Easy seat booking and cancellation Admin control panel for efficient management.

Our goal is to provide a transparent, reliable, and accessible digital reservation platform that keeps passengers at the heart of every feature.
